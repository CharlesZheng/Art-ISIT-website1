About Us

Arts ISIT

We take pride in providing exemplary technological support for the administrative, teaching, and learning needs of Arts units. Working collaboratively with other Arts and UBC support units, we strive to connect you and your communities with technological solutions that best meet your needs.

Job Descriptiopn

This position involves providing Tier 1 instructional support to Arts faculty, including troubleshooting and training in educational technologies, clear communication, task organization, and referrals to Arts ISIT. Preference is given to candidates who can commit for 12 months. Training will be provided, but experience in customer service, educational technologies, and strong communication skills are required. Skills in technical writing, HTML/CSS, and multimedia software are advantageous.
